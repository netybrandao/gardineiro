Our Services

At Gardineiro, we offer a tailored selection of gardening services designed to help your outdoor space thrive all year round. Whether you're after a quick tidy-up or regular maintenance, our friendly and experienced team is here to make your garden healthier, neater, and more enjoyable.

Lawn Mowing

Maintain a neat, green, and well-kept lawn with our regular mowing service. We use quality equipment to ensure a clean cut every time, keeping your yard looking sharp and inviting.

Tree Trimming

Promote healthy tree growth and improve your garden's appearance with our professional trimming service. We carefully shape trees and remove overgrowth to keep everything safe and visually balanced.

Leaf Cleaning

We clear fallen leaves, branches, and garden debris to keep your outdoor space clean and tidy. It's the perfect service for a seasonal refresh or regular garden care.
